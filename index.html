• Click in the direction you want to guide the Three Blind Mice, they need your help to solve each room!
  • Clues and secrets are scattered all over! Use your magnifying glass to find them, they usually sparkle!
  • If you ever get stuck, press Q to ask the Crystal Ball for a hint.
  • Remember to save your progress in the Pause Menu regularly and before exiting the game.
